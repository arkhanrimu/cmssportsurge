The Technology Powering Your Stream

Adaptive Bitrate Streaming (ABR) is the technology that eliminates buffering. Streams indexed on SportSurge utilize ABR technology, which supports up to 14 resolution steps (from 240p to 4K). Content Delivery Network (CDN) integration is the defining factor for speed. Premium streams utilize global CDNs, where servers cache video data physically closer to the user to reduce latency.
